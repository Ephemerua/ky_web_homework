DID YOU KNOW?

Food consumption is the second biggest contributor to greenhouse gas emissions. GHG emissions from transportation account for only a small part of food emissions and what you eat is far more important than where your food traveled from.

WHAT CAN YOU DO

Changing the foods that you eat can have a big impact. A vegetarian diet greatly reduces an individual’s carbon footprint. Our website sells low carbon meals, encouraging you to eat low carbon diets more and living a sustainable lifestyle.

OUR VISION

We encourage you to calculate your food emission. Learning about the health impact of different foods will influence the choices you make. Our food carbon footprint calculator can estimate the emissions of a meal and help you understand your carbon footprint.
